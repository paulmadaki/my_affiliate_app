How This Platform Works

Everything you need to know to get the most out of Rewards

1️⃣
Create Account

Sign up with your email, WhatsApp number, and location. It takes less than a minute.

2️⃣
Activate for $2

Pay a one-time activation fee of $2 (billed in Naira). This unlocks the game and withdrawal features.

3️⃣
Play Trivia Daily

You get 5 free chances every day to answer trivia questions. Answer correctly and win an instant airtime PIN.

4️⃣
Refer & Earn

Share your referral link. Earn $0.50 for every person who signs up and activates.

Every day at midnight your chances automatically reset to 5. Each time you submit an answer — whether correct or incorrect — one chance is used. When your chances reach zero, you must wait until the next day. Your chances counter at the top of this page always shows how many you have left for today.

A correct answer earns you an instant airtime recharge PIN. The PIN is displayed on screen immediately — copy it and load it directly on your phone. Each question can only be won once across all users, so speed matters.

Copy your unique referral link and share it with friends. When someone signs up through your link and pays the $2 activation fee, you earn $0.50 automatically added to your balance. Bonus: if you refer at least one person on any given day, you get unlimited game chances for that entire day.

Once your balance reaches $5.00 the withdrawal section appears. A 10% processing fee is deducted, so a $5.00 withdrawal pays out $4.50. Requests are processed within 24–48 hours.

  • Refer at least one person daily to unlock unlimited game chances for that day.
  • Play early in the day — airtime PINs are limited and claimed on a first-correct-answer basis.
  • Share your referral link on WhatsApp, social media, and to friends — every activation earns you $0.50.
  • Join the WhatsApp community to get notified when new PIN stock is added.
  • Check back daily — your 5 chances reset every midnight automatically.
